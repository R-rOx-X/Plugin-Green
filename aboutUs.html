Our Story

Founded in 2011 in Sehore City, Madhya Pradesh, Plugin-Green is a partnership firm that has set its foot as an electrical installation and trading leader.

Over the years, we have earned a reputation for excellence and professionalism. We continuously strive to meet and exceed standards to set the climate for sustainable business relationships with our clients.

As an electrical solution specialist, we offer power products ranging from transformers, switchgears, and breakers to XLPE cables and lighting fixtures. And now with Your overwhelming support we are stepping in the domain of EV and Solar Energy

Our Mission

To be the best-in-class provider of top-notch electrical products and services, harnessing the full potential of electricity as the cornerstone of high-quality life.

Our Vision

To establish long-term, mutually rewarding relationships by providing the highest quality products and expanding our facilities to serve customers with utmost professionalism.
